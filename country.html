My Country: Pakistan

About Pakistan

Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country with a population exceeding 225 million people. Pakistan shares borders with India to the east, Afghanistan and Iran to the west, and China to the northeast.

Capital

Islamabad

Largest City

Karachi

Official Languages

Urdu, English

Independence

August 14, 1947

Geography

Pakistan has diverse geography that includes:

  • The fertile Indus River plain in the east
  • The rugged mountains of the Hindu Kush and Himalayas in the north
  • The Balochistan Plateau in the west
  • The Thar Desert in the southeast

Economy

Pakistan is a developing country with a semi-industrialized economy. Major sectors include:

  • Agriculture (cotton, wheat, rice)
  • Textiles (Pakistan is the 8th largest exporter of textile products)
  • IT services
  • Manufacturing

Tourist Attractions

Pakistan has many beautiful places to visit:

  • Northern areas like Hunza Valley and Skardu
  • Historical sites like Mohenjo-Daro and Taxila
  • Mughal architecture in Lahore
  • Beautiful beaches in Gwadar
